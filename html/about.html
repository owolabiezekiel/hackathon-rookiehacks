About Us

Welcome to True Networking, your number one platform for entry level job seekers. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our services with a focus on community, jobs and stories.

Founded in 2020 by Megan Thong, Chelsea Alcinord and Tobiloba Owolabi, True Networking has come a long way from its beginnings in the 2020 RookieHacks Hackathon. As college students, we understand the hardships of finding entry-level jobs, insterships and the stress of working. We aim to create a supportive community, share success stories from users to inspire frustrated job-seekers, forums where job-seekers can ask for and give advice and guidance. We hope that True Networking would be the place that would introduce our users to new jobs, find comfort in others' stories and make someone's life better. We hope you will enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
