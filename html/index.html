Just graduated? Recently entered the job market? If you need help breaking into any industry, find your true network here.

Building a Supportive Community

With carefully moderated forums, we ensure that all posts and comments are of a friendly and supportive nature. This all goes to further developing a warm, caring community where job seekers can turn to for advice and friendship

Helping our Users Get Jobs

Our weekly blog features user success stories, inspiring other users to never give up in their job search. We also work to connect to those who have successfully gotten jobs with those still searching

Carefully Curated Resources

In our resources page, you can find links to other job sites and postings that you may find useful, and that have been proven to be useful for our users. We welcome new additions, just send us an email!

Our Community

It can feel like you're alone in your job search, and that everyone around you is getting jobs, however that is not the case. At True Networking, you will meet others going through the same job search experience as you. Support and encourage each other through the rejections and setbacks!

Helping You Get a Job

Too scared to ask for help on LinkedIn? Worried that Quora and Reddit aren't professional ways to get advice? Here is the platform for you, where you can ask your questions, and get answers from professionals who have gone through the same job search process as you, and who will not laugh at you no matter how stupid you think your question is.

Our Resources

In your job search, you have probably been inundated with new information, be it resources, websites, or magazines you 'must' read in order to get a job. All this information can sometimes make it harder for you to identify what's actually important. In our Resources section, we make sure that only what matters gets through to you.
